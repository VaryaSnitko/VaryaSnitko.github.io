Pandemic influenced the way we live, work, learn and communicate with each other. This video was made up to support all students who is quaranting or have to stay at home due to the pandemic. We tried to be positive and strive for self-betterment, showing the routine of the students while remote learning in a humorous way. After all, apart from the physical health of the person, the mental health is also pivotal in maintaining positive lifestyle. It is always a good iedea to pause and reflect on the changes that we are facing at the moment. Reflection and recognition of the struggles is a signifacnt step forward in the fight against physical and psychological hardships caused by the measures taken against the virus. Thus, we highly encourage you to wear masks, practice phisical distancing, and wash your hands frequently. But also do not forget that your mental wellbeing matters, and you have to take care of it. We hope that our short, funny video will make you a laugh at least a bit and approach the struggles caused by these circumstances more positivly.
